About Me

I grew up and spent many formative years in the San Francisco Bay Area, moved up to Oregon after high school for college and after living in Oregon for 6 years, I moved to Los Angeles to become an actor.

Just kidding. I never did want to become an actor, but the industry has always called to me. Growing up watching Fantasia with my parents, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles with my older brother, and going to Universal Studios Hollywood on vacations piqued my interest in animation and film from a young age.

Since then my career has taken me everywhere BUT the entertainment industry! I spent years working with children in classrooms and after-school programs, coached California state champion high school swimmers, spent half a decade working on an ambulance as an EMT running 911 calls, and even managed a busy boutique gym after all of that.

The truth is, through all of these adventures, my childhood interest never left me. So, for the last 9 months I've been working on making a career change into the Production/Administrative side of animation and film. My path to this point hasn't always been direct and my work experience is a little different from most, however, my attention to detail, strong interpersonal and organizational skills, and calm under pressure will translate seamlessly into the fast paced environment of the film and animation industry.

I read voraciously, I work out with my wife (who I mostly try to keep up with), and spend my evenings watching the next best recommended movie with a cat on my lap.
